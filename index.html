About Me

Hi, I'm Maddox 👋

My name is Robert Winchester, but I go by Maddox. I've been drawn to computers and technology for as long as I can remember, and that curiosity led me to Sprayberry's STEM program, where I've been able to explore engineering, design, and computer science in a hands-on environment.

Outside the classroom, I've toured tech companies with my cousin Allison — who has worked at places like Microsoft — getting a real look at what careers in technology look like from the inside. I've also worked summers at my dad's design shop, picking up skills in car wrapping, signage, and running a creative business. I'm currently a member of TSA and the National Honor Society, and I'm headed to Kennesaw State University to study Computer Engineering.
